Physical therapy clinics serving Brandon, Pinellas Park and Lakeland Florida.

Our Physical Therapy Practice specializes in the prevention and treatment of a variety of challenging musculoskeletal conditions in people who have not found solutions through conventional means. Some of these conditions may be: chronic lower back pain and neck pain, headaches, dizziness, whiplash, repetitive stress disorders, work and sport related injuries. Our care is unique, and focused not only on rehabilitating your condition, but preventing future dysfunction from occurring. Your care will be based on the latest in research based physical therapy, manual therapy, sports medicine, exercise therapy, as well as occupational health and ergonomic studies.
