Joint Formula/Joint Tri-Basic

Targeted Joint and Connective Tissue Support


Joint Tri-Basic™ features targeted nutritional support for joints and connective tissue.* This formula has been specifically designed to promote healthy cartilage strength and resilience.* It contains glucosamine, a natural compound found in all body tissues, which is the fundamental building block required for the synthesis of glycosaminoglycans such as chondroitin sulfate.* Chondroitin sulfate is the primary structural compound of cartilage and helps block the activity of damaging enzymes.* Glucosamine and chondroitin sulfate play a synergistic role in supporting healthy hyaluronic acid content and synovial fluid.* Additionally, MSM provides support for optimal cartilage matrix composition, connective tissue strength and joint comfort.*
